Overview

The State Bank of India (SBI) boasts a rich history that dates back to the early 19th century, with the establishment of the Bank of Calcutta in Calcutta on June 2, 1806. This institution later evolved into the Bank of Bengal on January 2, 1809, becoming the first joint-stock bank in British India, backed by the Government of Bengal. In subsequent years, the Bank of Bombay (founded on April 15, 1840) and the Bank of Madras (established on July 1, 1843) joined the banking landscape. These three banks played a pivotal role in modern Indian banking until they merged into the Imperial Bank of India on January 27, 1921.


SBI's history reflects its organic growth, shaped by the financial demands of the British imperial administration and local European commerce. Drawing inspiration from European and English banking practices, it adapted to changes in the local trade environment and the evolving relationship between the Indian economy and the global economic landscape. Recognizing the significance of this historical foundation, SBI decided to create an Experience Center at its head office in Colaba, Mumbai. This center aims not only to showcase the bank's history but also to provide a unique and immersive learning experience.

Requirements

To bring the bank's rich history to life, the Experience Center at SBI's Colaba head office required a range of interactive and visually engaging elements. These included

  • Interactive Walls: Walls equipped with interactive displays, allow visitors to explore the bank's history, milestones, and achievements engagingly.
  • Touch Panels: Touch-sensitive screens that provide a hands-on experience for visitors to delve deeper into the bank's historical journey.
  • Immersive 2D & 3D Graphics: The use of compelling 2D and 3D graphics to visually narrate the bank's history and evolution, creating an immersive experience for visitors.

Services Offered

The Experience Center aimed to provide a range of services, including

Graphic Concept & Digital Artworks: Detailed and visually appealing digital artworks were created to showcase the journey of SBI, effectively narrating the bank's role as a witness to the evolution of India's economic infrastructure.

Solution

The creation of an Experience Center at SBI's Colaba head office provided a unique solution to celebrate the bank's historical legacy. The interactive walls, touch panels, and 2D & 3D graphics transformed the historical narrative into an engaging and informative experience.


Visitors, whether they are banking enthusiasts, students, or curious customers, can now embark on a journey through time to understand how SBI's evolution has been closely intertwined with India's economic development. It is a tribute to an institution that has stood as a stalwart witness to the economic transformation of India.
